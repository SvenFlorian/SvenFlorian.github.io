How to compete in memory

In progress.

Some vocabulary:
Memorization time - the time you get to memorize the information
Recall time - the time you get to write down and recall the information
SR - Swedish Record
WR - World Record
Formula - how to calculate the score of an event

1

5 min words

Memorization time: 5 min
Recall time: 15 min
SR: 95, Jonas von Essen
WR: 124, Simon Reinhard
Formula: 1000 x (n / 125)

2

5 min binary

Memorization time: 5 min
Recall time: 15 min
SR: 962, Jonas von Essen
WR: 1080, Johannes Mallow
Formula: 1000 x (n / 1000)

3

5 min names & faces

Memorization time: 5 min
Recall time: 15 min
SR: 59, Jonas von Essen
WR: 83, Simon Reinhard
Formula: 1000 x (n / 70)

4

15 min numbers


Memorization time: 15 min
Recall time: 45 min
SR: 764, Jonas von Essen
WR: 937, Johannes Mallow
Formula: 1000 x (n / 900)

5

10 min cards

Memorization time: 10 min
Recall time: 30 min
SR: 319, Jonas von Essen

Formula: 1000 x (n / 364)

6

5 min numbers
